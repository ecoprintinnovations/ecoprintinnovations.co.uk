Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer different solutions for different sectors?

Absolutely! We customize our approach based on your sector's specific needs. Educational projects focus on curriculum alignment and safety, while commercial projects emphasize durability and functionality. Arts and cultural projects prioritize aesthetic quality and creative expression.

Can you help with educational curriculum integration?

Yes! We specialize in helping schools and universities integrate 3D printing into their STEM curriculum. We provide lesson plans, project ideas, and training tailored to different age groups and educational levels.

What if I need both design and printing services?

We offer complete end-to-end solutions. Many of our clients start with just an idea, and we handle everything from concept development to final production. Our team will guide you through each step of the process.

Do you provide on-site training and maintenance?

Yes, we offer both on-site and remote services depending on your needs. For schools and businesses, we often conduct on-site training workshops. Maintenance and repair services are available both remotely and on-site within reasonable travel distance.
