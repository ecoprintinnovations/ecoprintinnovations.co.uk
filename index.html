Frequently Asked Questions

What file types do you accept for 3D printing?

We accept STL, OBJ, and STEP files. If you have a different format, just ask—our team can usually convert it for you!

Can you help me design a 3D model from scratch?

Absolutely! Our in-house design team loves bringing ideas to life. Share your concept, and we’ll work with you to design and print it.

Do you offer bulk or business discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk orders and business clients. Contact us for a tailored quote.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Most orders are completed and shipped within 3–7 days, depending on complexity and volume. We’ll always keep you updated!
