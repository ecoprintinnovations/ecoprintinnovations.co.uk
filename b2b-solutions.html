B2B services

Short, clear descriptions aligned to your briefs and deadlines.

Rapid Prototyping (Functional & Visual)

Fast iterations for fit checks, testing and presentation models.

Custom Jigs & Fixtures

Alignment, drilling guidance, assembly and inspection aids for repeatable results.

Component Protection (Caps, Covers & Transit Guards)

Protective parts to reduce damage in handling, storage and shipping.

Tooling Aids & Workshop Tools

Guides, templates, holders, racks and assembly aids built for the shop floor.

Small-Batch Production (3D Printed Parts)

Consistent short runs for non-safety-critical components and tooling parts.

Design for Additive Consultation (DFAM)

Improve strength, printability, lead time and cost with practical design advice.

Product Development Support (Concept to Prototype)

Turn ideas into workable prototypes and refine quickly.

Film & Creative Props (Bespoke Builds)

Lightweight, paint-ready props/set pieces made to spec on tight deadlines.

Replacement & Obsolescence Parts (Non-OEM)

Recreate functional equivalents from measurements/photos/samples (non-branded).

Eco-Focused Materials & Sustainable Printing

Where suitable, eco-conscious materials and low-waste print strategy.

How it works

A straightforward process designed for B2B briefs.

Send your brief

3

4

Prototype / build

Deliver
